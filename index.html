What is warwickCoding?

Our mission is to provide an interesting and affordable access to the world of coding, we present to you a chance to learn a skill set unique to only a few but desired by many

Founded in 2014

After a successful first year, we are hoping to give another group of students the foundations on which they can build their coding skills

127 Student reached

In one year we were able to teach 127 students how to build a website and we aim to give as many people the skills to achieve their goals this year

Course Diversity

Whether you want to learn to build a website or an app we have a wide variety of courses for students to choose from

Gender Ratio

We are one of the few coding courses that have more than 50% female students

Growing Number of Courses

Last year we only offered courses on website building but this year we have expanded and we now offer a course on Android and Ruby development

Affordable Courses

We want all of the courses to be affordable to every student. With that in mind, we never price any of our courses over £70
